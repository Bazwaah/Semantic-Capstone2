About Me

I’m Barry — a full-stack web dev in training, passionate about crafting fast, accessible, and responsive websites. Whether I’m refining layouts in Tailwind or diving deep into semantic HTML, I thrive on solving problems and learning new tricks.

I enjoy transforming designs into clean, performant code and experimenting with tools like Supabase, Next.js, and GitHub to bring projects to life.

When I’m not coding, I’m exploring the digital wilds — improving workflows, restructuring legacy projects, and occasionally debugging with sheer willpower alone.
